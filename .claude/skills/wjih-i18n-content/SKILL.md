# WJIH i18n Content

Use this skill to manage Arabic and English content safely.

## Language Rules

* Arabic is the primary language.
* English is supported through the language switch.
* Arabic must use RTL.
* English must use LTR.
* Keep `Soft Landing` in English when appropriate.

## Content Rules

* Do not change approved meaning.
* Do not invent important claims.
* Prefer a concise, professional, community/startup-hub tone.
* If translating, preserve meaning rather than literal wording.
* Long content should live in locale or content files, not scattered in JSX.
* Keep navigation labels, CTA labels, and page copy translated.
* Keep content organized so a future dashboard or CMS can replace the content layer later.

## Verification Checklist

* Arabic and English keys exist.
* No missing translation keys.
* Direction changes correctly.
* Navigation labels are translated.
* CTA labels are translated.
* Meaning is preserved.
